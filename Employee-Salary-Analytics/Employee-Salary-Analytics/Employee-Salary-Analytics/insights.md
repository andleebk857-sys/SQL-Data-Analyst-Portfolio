# Employee Salary Analytics – Key Insights

## 1. Top Earners per Department
- Each department has 1–2 employees who earn significantly more than others.
- These employees may be key performers or senior roles.
- Management can review whether salary distribution is balanced.

---

## 2. Employees Above Department Average
- Multiple employees earn above their department’s average salary.
- This helps identify high-value employees and potential promotion candidates.
- Useful for performance reviews and compensation planning.

---

## 3. Salary Ranking Within Departments
- Ranking employees within departments provides transparency in pay structure.
- Helps managers understand internal salary gaps.
- Can highlight potential fairness or retention issues.

---

## 4. Department Salary Budget Analysis
- Some departments consume a larger share of the salary budget.
- Departments with high total salary and employee count may need cost optimization.
- Useful for annual budgeting and workforce planning.

---

## 5. Manager Salary Responsibility
- Managers handle different levels of salary budgets.
- Ranking managers by team salary highlights leadership responsibility.
- Can support workload balancing and performance evaluation.

---

## Business Impact
This analysis supports:
- Strategic salary planning
- Budget control
- Fair compensation policies
- Better managerial decision-making
